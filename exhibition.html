Vincent van Gogh has a warehouse full of paintings, 2 of which have the photo One of the more famous would be his self-portrait he made a self-portrait to see where his painting skills lie. Only one portrait photo of Vincent has survived: he is then 19 years old and looks a bit sullen. We also know especially how he looked, because he painted himself so many times. There are no fewer than 35 known self-portraits of him. It shows that he had red hair, green eyes and an angular face. Yet the faces are always different.

Second, we chose one of his most famous paintings: The Sunflowers. His paintings of sunflowers had special significance for Van Gogh. They expressed "gratitude," he wrote.
